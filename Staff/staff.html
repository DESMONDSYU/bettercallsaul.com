Meet Our Team

Saul Goodman

Attonery

Legal maverick Saul Goodman, a master of charm and dubious tactics, navigates the legal labyrinth. Your guide through the storm, Saul's flair and cunning ensure justice – for a price.


Meet Saul Goodman, the legal maestro who turns adversity into opportunity. With a personality as vibrant as his advertisements, Saul is more than just a lawyer; he's a force of nature. Armed with a razor-sharp wit and a deep understanding of the legal system, Saul navigates the complex world of law with finesse and flair.

Saul's journey to becoming Albuquerque's go-to lawyer wasn't conventional, but it's precisely what sets him apart. Before the flashy commercials and catchy slogans, Saul honed his skills in the trenches, handling cases that others deemed impossible. His unorthodox methods and creative problem-solving have earned him a reputation as the man to call when you're in a bind.

But behind the colorful exterior is a lawyer who gets results. Saul's commitment to justice, however unconventional, is unwavering. Whether you're facing criminal charges, a messy divorce, or a tricky business deal, Saul has the expertise to guide you through the legal labyrinth. Just remember, justice has a price, and Saul Goodman is ready to negotiate.

Francesca Liddy

Secretary

Saul's organizational wizard, orchestrates order amid chaos. Her memory, efficiency, and touch of sass make her the indispensable backbone of Goodman's legal escapades.


Francesca Liddy is the unsung hero behind Saul Goodman's chaotic charm. As the organizational dynamo of the operation, Francesca keeps the wheels turning smoothly in the face of legal madness. Her role is more than just managing appointments and paperwork; Francesca is the glue that holds the Goodman legal empire together.

With an impeccable memory and an eye for detail, Francesca ensures that no detail goes unnoticed. Whether you're a high-profile client or a frazzled intern, Francesca treats everyone with the same level of professionalism and a dash of sass. Need a document at a moment's notice or a reassuring voice on the other end of the line? Francesca is the heartbeat of Saul's office, ensuring that every client feels like they're in capable hands.

In a world where chaos reigns, Francesca brings order. Her ability to juggle multiple tasks with ease and maintain a cool composure under pressure makes her an invaluable asset to the Goodman team. Behind every successful lawyer is a formidable assistant, and in Saul's case, that's Francesca Liddy.

Huell Babineaux

Security

The gentle giant with a heart of gold, provides unwavering support in Saul's world. His towering presence and loyalty bring a unique charm to legal proceedings.


Meet Huell Babineaux, the gentle giant with a heart of gold. In Saul Goodman's world, where charisma meets chaos, Huell is the calming presence that anchors the team. Standing tall with an imposing stature, Huell's loyalty and warmth make him more than just a security detail – he's family.

Huell's journey with Saul began as a security guard, but his role quickly evolved into much more. Whether it's providing a watchful eye during tense legal negotiations or offering a well-timed quip to lighten the mood, Huell brings a unique charm to the legal proceedings. His unwavering support for Saul and the clients is a testament to his character.

Despite his intimidating appearance, Huell is approachable and compassionate. Clients and colleagues alike find solace in his presence. In a profession known for its cutthroat nature, Huell stands out as the embodiment of humanity. As Saul Goodman's right-hand man, Huell not only ensures the safety of the team but also adds a touch of heart to the often ruthless world of law.
